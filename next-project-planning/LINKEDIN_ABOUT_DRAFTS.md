## My honest recommendation

**Draft A.** Reasons:
1. The Wildlife Sentinel cold open is your single strongest differentiator. Putting it in the first sentence of your About means it lands even on a 5-second skim.
2. Recruiters in 2026 are tired of pivot stories from bootcamp grads. Draft B leads with one. Draft A leads with proof of work, then makes the pivot a supporting detail rather than the headline.
3. Draft C is beautiful but high-variance. It works if you're applying to teams who appreciate a writer; it underperforms with engineering managers who want to know what you ship in the first 100 words.

Draft B is a strong runner-up if you want to lean harder into the "TypeScript / non-traditional / Olympic Trials" combination as a memorability play.
